Corporate Connection

ACM members at the University of Illinois are some of the most skillful, dedicated computer science and engineering students in the world. The corporate relations committee and jobs mailing list make it easy for employers and sponsors to interact with ACM members.

Post a job ad

Your advertisement will automatically be included in the next issue of ACM's weekly jobs e-mail newsletter. This newsletter reaches over 600 top undergraduate and graduate students in Computer Science, Electrical and Computer Engineering, and related fields.

Contact ACM Corporate Committee

The Corporate Committee is ACM's central point of contact for all corporate relations activities. Please e-mail corporate@acm.uiuc.edu rather than an individual student to ensure that your message is properly archived and visible to the entire Corporate Committee.
