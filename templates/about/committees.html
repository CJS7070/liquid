ACM Committees

In addition to SIGs, ACM has a number of committees that work to support the SIGs and ACM as a whole. If you are interested in getting involved with one of these groups, please contact acm@uiuc.edu.

Corporate Liaisons

The corporate committee is responsible for maintaining ACM's contacts with our corporate partners. This is a tough job, but it's a great way to get your foot in the door with some of the industry's greatest companies.

NT and UNIX Admins

The ACM Cluster could always use more sysadmin-types to help keep things in line. Even if your skills are lacking, we can help you get started.
